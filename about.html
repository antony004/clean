About Cleanoritas

Home is your personal haven. A place where you should feel relaxed after a stressful work day (or any other stress-induced activity). This won't be achieved if you come home to a filthy, unorganized place; and that is why we are here.
With all new green products, our staff will make your home a peaceful environment. Not only do we help you relax but we also try to reduce our individual carbon footprint. Along with that, at the end of our fiscal year, we will donate 1% of our profits to UCS to support science-based solutions for a healthy, safe, and sustainable future.
Our friendly staff is trained and reliable. Whether you need a one-time clean or a weekly basis deal, you can count on us. Our service is guaranteed. If you think our staff missed something, we will go back until you're 100% satisfied.
